Trim Levels

The L Trim

The L trim is the base model of the Sorento.

• Apple CarPlay & Android Auto

• Bluetooth

• 7” touch-screen display

• Remote keyless entry

• Cloth seats with YES Essentials Fabric Treatment

• 17-inch alloy wheels

The S V6 Trim

The S V6 trim has additional interior, safety, and performance features.

• Blind spot collision avoidance-assist (BCA)

• Forward & rear cross-traffic collision avoidance

• 19-inch alloy wheels

• Heated front seats

• Leather wrapped steering wheel and gearshift knob

• Smart key w/ push-button start

The SX Trim

The most impressively equipped Sorento is the SX.

• 14-way power-adjustable driver’s seat

• Harman/Kardon 10-speaker surround sound audio

• Park distance warning – forward (PDW – F)

• Power-folding & reverse tilt-down outside mirrors

• UVO link w/ 8” touch-screen display

• Voice-command navigation system

• Wireless phone charger
